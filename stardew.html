Stardew Valley

"You've inherited your grandfather's old farm plot in Stardew Valley. Armed with hand-me-down tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life!"












This is one of my favorite games of all time. Though there are ones that are similar, such as Animal Crossing, Stardew Valley takes the cake in farming simulators. Stardew Valley is one single game that recieves updates to add new items to the games, while Animal Crossing is multiple different games that have some online features that allow you to play with others. Stardew Valley has a co-operative playing mode, while Animal Crossing does not. I think that there is a wider variety of things to do in Stardew Valley.
