The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

"In this sequel to the Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild game, you’ll decide your own path through the sprawling landscapes of Hyrule and the mysterious islands floating in the vast skies above. Can you harness the power of Link’s abilities to fight back against the malevolent forces that threaten the kingdom?"











Due to the large following that The Legend of Zelda has, there are many opinions out there. Below is a pros and cons list that covers some of the main discourse.

Pros Cons
The Legend of Zelda games have not had a direct sequel since 2015 and many fans were delighted that a game would be made in the same format as BOTW. TOTK took place on the exact same map as BOTW, just with a fresh skin. Many people felt that there was not enough new content to differentiate the maps.
The new game had many new features available through a plethora of side-quests, providing players with countless more hours of gameplay. Many players felt that these new features did not break away from the standards that BOTW had set. It seemed more like unsubstantial busywork.
In addition to the well-known map of Hyrule, three new major features were added. Throughout the land, there are now caves to explore. Above in the sky, there are floating islands that have suddenly appeared. Below your feet, the Depths are discovered. All in all, these three locations had very little to introduce and it quickly became redundant to explore them. Most enemies in the depths were re-skinned versions of ones you find on the surface. The sky and caves had a couple mobs that were new to the BOTW map, but even some of those had been seen in previous games.
